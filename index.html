About Gemini

From a small team of believers to a major producer of freshly roasted coffee and tea.

Gemini grew from a compact team of like-minded people into a large-scale producer of natural freshly roasted coffee and tea. Today, the brand develops blends for every brewing method, single-origin selections, specialty coffee, ready-to-drink beverages, drip coffee, and a broad line of premium natural tea.

Gemini works according to Fair Trade principles by purchasing beans directly from farmers at an honest price. Once the coffee arrives at production, the team roasts, cups, evaluates, and calibrates each lot to find the optimal roast profile for every variety.

The result is a brand with genuine industrial capability and strong product culture: roast, pack, blend, serve, train, install, maintain, and help clients scale.
