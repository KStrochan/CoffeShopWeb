About the company

Coffee is our responsibility and our passion.

Gemini started as a small team of like-minded people and grew into a large producer of natural freshly roasted coffee and tea. The brand works according to Fair Trade principles, buying beans directly from farmers at a fair price.

Gemini roasts coffee at its own production facility in Kyiv region using modern Italian equipment, creates blends for different brewing methods, develops single-origin and specialty lines, serves both retail and business clients, and supports cafés, restaurants, offices, hotels, fuel stations, and retail networks.
